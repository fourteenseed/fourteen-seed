An AI studio >> Building things that work in the real world.

I'm Wendy Harris. I work alongside businesses and individuals who want AI doing something useful in their day-to-day, not just in a demo. Embedded, hands-on, time-bounded.

Co-founded a SaaS company in 2011. Exited 2025. Fourteen years helping hospitality businesses go from paper to digital. What's happening with AI is the biggest shift in how businesses reach people since the internet itself, and I'm building for it. I work with the tools you already have, or help you choose some.
