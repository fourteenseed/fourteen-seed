What I focus on

Most hospitality businesses don't have one big problem. They have 50 small ones quietly costing them money. I focus on finding and fixing them:

  • Revenue leakage across departments
  • Enquiry handling and follow-up that falls through the gaps
  • Pre-arrival guest journeys that are manual, inconsistent, or nonexistent
  • Fragmented systems (PMS, bookings, comms, reporting) that don't talk to each other
  • Management time eaten by reporting that could be automated

My approach

I don't start with tools. I start with operations.

  1. Map where time is lost and revenue leaks
  2. Identify the repeatable workflows
  3. Layer AI into those workflows
  4. Keep humans in control with clear guardrails

AI should feel like a competent colleague, not a risky experiment.
