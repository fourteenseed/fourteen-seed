We're all learning

I put this cohort together because I think the fastest way through the Anthropic certification is alongside people who are already doing the work, not just reading about it. This site is our shared reference: the roadmap, the qualification detail, and the people in the room.

May is foundation month. The free Anthropic Academy courses are where we start, and I'd like everyone to finish them before June. Not because they're difficult, but because arriving at the exam modules with that grounding makes a real difference. The first certification domain is a noticeable step up, and the gap shows if you've skipped the basics.

If you've already done Claude 101 and AI Fluency, go back and pick up anything you glossed over. If you haven't started, start now. We move into exam prep as a group in June, and I want us starting from the same place.

May

Free courses, learning together

Work through the free Anthropic Academy courses at your own pace. Claude 101 and AI Fluency are the starting point. The cohort WhatsApp group is where we share what's clicking, ask questions, and keep each other honest. Use it.

June and July

CCAF modules, exam prep

We move into the five exam domains as a group: structured weekly focus, peer sessions, and a shared target to sit the exam before the end of July. The qualification breakdown and prep timeline are on the next page.

The free courses are at anthropic.com/learn. No fee, no prerequisite: just an email address and a Skilljar account. Claude 101 and AI Fluency are the two to start with, in that order.
