Five critique rounds

OriginBrian Proffitt · shared in Nate's AI Exec Circle

A structured five-round critique framework for improving any piece of work, from a newsletter draft to a strategy document. Each round applies a different lens so you move through the work systematically, rather than letting Claude jump to surface-level edits before the structure is solid. Works on skills, documents, pitches, plans, product copy, or anything else you want to stress-test before it goes out.

The five rounds

01

Structure and flow

Does this hold together as a whole?

02

Depth and specificity

Is this concrete enough to be useful?

03

Edge cases and failure modes

What could go wrong?

04

Cross-references and consistency

Does this agree with itself?

05

Polish and readability

Is this a pleasure to read?
