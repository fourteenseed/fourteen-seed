gaining the qualification

Claude Certified Architect Foundations

CCAF (also referred to as CCA-F) is Anthropic's first architecture-level technical certification. It tests whether you can make sound design decisions when building production applications with Claude: orchestration, tool integration, context management, structured output, and reliability under real-world conditions.

For this cohort, CCAF matters because it gives the work a shared standard. Passing it is a meaningful signal: you've been tested, closed-book, on the decisions that separate a working system from one that fails at scale. It maps well to what we're all building, agentic workflows, multi-step reasoning, and the practical question of how to make Claude reliably useful.

The five exam domains

The exam draws from five weighted domains, each one mapping to a real challenge in production AI development. Together they account for 100% of the exam score. The sequence below follows exam weighting, largest first.

01  ·  largest domain

Agentic architecture and orchestration

Designing multi-agent systems that actually work: the Agent SDK, hub-and-spoke topologies, lifecycle hooks, and how to structure orchestration so that complex tasks complete reliably even when individual steps fail or branch unexpectedly.

27% of exam
02

Prompt engineering and structured output

Context engineering, JSON schemas, extraction patterns, and few-shot techniques. The craft of getting Claude to produce exactly what your system needs, in exactly the format it requires, consistently and without manual cleanup.

20% of exam
03

Claude Code configuration and workflows

CLAUDE.md, agent skills, plan mode, slash commands, and CI/CD integration. How to configure Claude Code for real development environments and wire it into production workflows so it behaves consistently across a team.

20% of exam
04

Tool design and MCP integration

Building MCP servers, defining tool boundaries, working with resources and prompts, and making decisions about when and how to expose capabilities to Claude. The layer between Claude and the systems it needs to touch.

18% of exam
05

Context management and reliability

Long-context strategies, multi-agent handoffs, error propagation, and escalation paths. How to build systems that degrade gracefully, recover without losing work, and keep a human meaningfully in the loop when something unexpected happens.

15% of exam

Exam format

Delivered online through Anthropic Academy via Skilljar. Closed-book, timed, no AI assistance of any kind during the exam.

Duration 120 minutes
Questions 60 multiple-choice. One correct answer, three distractors. Four of six scenarios selected at random.
Format Closed-book. No AI assistance permitted.
Passing score 720 on a scaled score of 100 to 1,000
Where to sit Anthropic Academy, registered via Skilljar
Cost $99 (approximately £80 at current rates). Free for the first 5,000 Claude Partner Network employees.
