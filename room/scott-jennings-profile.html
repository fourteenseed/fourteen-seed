Building IMI, decision infrastructure that turns meeting transcripts into a living knowledge graph your AI tools can actually use. Decisions get made, then get forgotten or contradicted. IMI catches that.

Based in Hillsborough, NC. Here via Nate's AI Exec Circle. Building since 2025, with a first real-data pilot underway with an executive search firm.

What I'm building

IMI ingests transcripts from whatever recording tool you already use and builds a "decision constitution" — the decisions, commitments, and rules your organisation has established, monitored for drift. The problem it solves is simple and persistent: decisions get made in rooms, then drift, get forgotten, or get contradicted six months later by people who weren't there. IMI makes that visible before it becomes expensive.
